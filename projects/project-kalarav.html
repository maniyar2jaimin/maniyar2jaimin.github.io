Kalarav - The Step Towards Baby Care

B.E. Final Year Project

It is website which is developed in J2EE with mysql. This projects aim is to help the parents of new born baby. This project help the parents start from a lady become pregnant to child become 10 years.

This projects help parents in all faces e.g. if any problem during pregnancy or after pregnancy period there is a discussion forum, Blog & live chat with doctor to help them to solve the problem. There is functionality of online baby product store. The product store have a feature of one click buy and buy on every requested interval automatically. This project also have functionality of sharing of things related to baby. etc functionalities are there.
